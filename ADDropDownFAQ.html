Why is air changes per hour (ACH) important?
Experts recommend 5 air changes per hour for optimal removal of airborne particles from the air because if you’re not moving the air, you’re not cleaning the air. You can rest assured that AirDoctor purifiers are powerful enough to clean the air 5 times per hour, or every 12 minutes, for their respective room size coverage to deliver you pure, protected air.

How often do I need to change the filters?
Based on 24/7 operation, the Carbon/VOC filter should be replaced every 6 months and the UltraHEPA™ filter should be replaced every 12 months. We recommend replacing filters when your Filter Change Alert indicator light signals for a filter replacement.
